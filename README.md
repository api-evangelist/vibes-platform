# Vibes Platform (vibes-platform)
Vibes Platform is a social media marketing tool that helps businesses enhance their online presence and engage with their target audience in a meaningful way. By providing a platform for creating and scheduling visually appealing social media posts, Vibes Platform allows users to easily manage their social media accounts and track the impact of their content. With features such as analytics and audience insights, businesses can better understand their online community and tailor their social media strategy for maximum effectiveness. Overall, Vibes Platform empowers businesses to build stronger connections with their customers and drive growth through social media marketing.

### Vibes Platform
To send a message to your users, you will need to create a broadcast. A broadcast can refer to either a SMS message or a push notification and is delivered to subscribers within your mobile contact book.
